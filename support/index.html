About HWHP-High

HWHP-High is an educational quiz and practice app for high-school students. The app includes timed questions, practice mode, hints, solutions, progress tracking, subject unlocks, and achievement certificates.

Common Support Topics

  • Help with student, parent, or teacher email setup.
  • Questions about progress reports or certificate emails.
  • Issues with subject unlocks or in-app purchases.
  • Questions about saved progress, recent scores, or practice history.
  • Bug reports or app performance issues.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. If you experience a billing issue, first check your Apple purchase history or contact Apple Support. For app-specific unlock issues after a successful purchase, email us with a brief description of the problem.
