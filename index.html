Privacy Policy for HWHP-High

Last updated: May 31, 2026

HWHP-High is an educational quiz and practice app for high-school students. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and how users can contact us about privacy questions.

Information We Collect

The app may collect or store the following information when provided or created during app use:

  • Student name.
  • Student, parent, and teacher email addresses.
  • Subject practice activity, including subjects used, access counts, time spent, and recent scores.
  • Certificate information, such as the student's name, subject, level, and completion date.
  • Optional images selected or captured in the app for profile, avatar, pet, astronaut, or certificate features.
  • Purchase-related status, such as unlock points and unlocked subjects.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide the app's educational features, including:

  • Saving student progress and unlocked subjects.
  • Showing recent scores and practice history.
  • Creating and sending progress reports to configured student, parent, or teacher email addresses.
  • Creating and sending achievement certificates when a student passes a subject level.
  • Supporting app purchases and subject unlocks.

Email Reports and Certificates

If email addresses are provided in the app, HWHP-High may send progress reports and certificates to the configured student, parent, teacher, or recipient email addresses. These emails may include student name, subject activity, scores, practice time, and certificate details.

Camera and Photos

The app may allow users to choose or capture images for profile, pet, astronaut, or certificate features. These images are used inside the app experience and may be included in certificate-related features when selected by the user.

In-App Purchases

HWHP-High may offer in-app purchases for unlock points or related educational content access. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store full payment card information.

Data Storage

App progress, profile details, selected images, unlock status, and related app data may be stored locally on the user's device. Some information may be sent by email when users use progress report or certificate email features.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to operate app features, such as sending emails to the addresses configured by the user or processing purchases through Apple.

Children's Privacy

HWHP-High is intended for educational use by students, parents, and teachers. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information and wants it reviewed or removed, please contact us.

Data Deletion

Users can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from the device. For privacy questions or requests related to emailed information, contact us using the email address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
